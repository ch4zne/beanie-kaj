About Us

"At Beanie KAJ, we believe in crafting coffee that warms your heart. Founded with a passion for quality and community, our shop is the perfect place to relax and connect."

A calming, delightful, great ambiance of Beanie KAJ were introduced on November 2024. The friendship of three
owners: Jorilyne P. Lanuzo, Kc Cassandra Salvador, and Angelica Chazne Vasquez were bond together
to give a try on making their own brewed espresso using ground coffee beans, and that's how
it went started this entire cafe.

Beanie KAJ came from our combined names. K for Kc, A for Angelica, and J for Jor. It was created to give every customers a full
relaxing and worth it moment inside our cafe. This cafe brings us joy, luck, worthy, and charm that may lead us to be a successful ones.
